# **PREVENTION CHECKLIST**

**Stay Safe Online Checklist**

* Never approve unknown UPI payment requests
* Do not share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone
* Always verify the sender before making any transaction
* Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
* Regularly check your bank statements for unusual activity
* Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store/App Store
* For UPI safety: Always remember — receiving money does NOT require PIN
* For UPI safety: Never scan unknown QR codes for receiving money
* Report fraud immediately on National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
* Call helpline 1930 for quick action
