## Project Overview

This project has been developed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The objective is to understand and apply essential digital skills required for academic and professional growth. As a Student Digital Ambassador, this project focuses on promoting responsible, safe, and effective use of digital tools and platforms.

The project is divided into five tasks, each addressing a specific aspect of digital literacy, including awareness, professional presence, platform usage, communication, and cybersecurity.


## Tasks Summary

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic  
A one-page infographic was created using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. It covers key areas such as digital tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.



### Task 2 – Digital Portfolio  
Professional profiles were created and updated on GitHub and LinkedIn. These platforms are used to build a digital identity, showcase skills, and connect with professionals.


### Task 3 – Platforms Exploration  
A beginner-level coding problem was solved on HackerRank to understand coding platforms. Additionally, a Google Form quiz on digital literacy was created to demonstrate the use of collaboration tools.



### Task 4 – Email Etiquette  
Two formal emails were drafted to demonstrate proper email communication. A checklist outlining appropriate and inappropriate social media behavior was also prepared.



### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness  
A case study on UPI-based fraud was developed to highlight common cyber threats. A prevention checklist was also created to promote safe online practices.
